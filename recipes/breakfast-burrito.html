Minute Breakfast Burrito

Breakfast wrap containing egg, salsa and cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 2 Tablespoons of Salsa
  • 1 Slice of American Cheese
  • 1 Tortilla

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Spray cereal bowl with cooking spray. Crack eggs, add salsa and stir.
  3. Microwave on high for 1 minute, stir and cook for another minute until firm.
  4. Place cheese in the center of tortilla and top with egg mix.
  5. Roll up and enjoy!
