Simple Sweet and Spicy Chicken Wraps

Spicy chicken wrap flavored with mayo, honey and cucumber.

Ingredients:

Sauce:

  • 1/2 Cup of Mayonnaise
  • 1/4 Cup of Chopped Cucumber
  • 1 Tablespoon of Honey
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Cayenne Pepper
  • Ground Black Pepper

Wraps:

  • 2 Tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 1 1/2 Pounds of Boneless Chicken Breast Halves Cut Into Thin Strips
  • 1 Cup of Thick and Chunky Salsa
  • 1 Tablespoon of Honey
  • 1/2 Teaspoons of Cayenne Pepper
  • 8 (10 inch) Flour Tortillas
  • 1 (10 ounce) Bag of Baby Spinach Leaves

Steps:

Sauce:

  1. Mix mayonaise, cucumber, honey, cayenne, and black pepper in a bowl until smooth. Cover and refrigerate.

Wraps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet on medium-high. Add chicken strips, cook and stir until they start to turn golden are no longer pink in the middle (about 8 minutes). Stir in salsa, honey and cayenne pepper. Reduce heat to medium-low, simmer and occasionally stir until flavors have been blended (about 5 minutes).
  2. Stack tortillas 4 at a time in a microwave and heat until warm (for about 30 seconds per batch).
  3. Spread each tortilla with 1 tablespoon mayonnaise-cucumber sauce, top with a layer of spinach and arrange about 1/2 cup of chicken mixture on top.
  4. Fold the bottom of each tortilla up about 2 inches, and start rolling from the right side. When the wrap is halfway rolled, fold the top of the tortilla down, enclosing the filling and continue rolling to make a tight compact cylinder.
