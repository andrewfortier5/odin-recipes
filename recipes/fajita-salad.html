Grilled Chicken Fajita Salad

Hot grilled fajita salad.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Pound of Skinless Boneless Chicken Breasts Cut Into Strips
  • 1 (12 ounce) Bottle of Liquid Fajita Marinade
  • 2 Teaspoons of Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Minced Clove of Garlic
  • 1/2 Teaspoons of Salt
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Ground Black Pepper
  • 2 Heads of Romaine Lettuce
  • 2 Red Bell Peppers Cut Into 1/4 Inch Strips
  • 1/2 Onion Cut Into 1/4 Inch Strips

Steps:

  1. Combine chicken strips and fajita marinade in a bowl. Let marinate in the refrigerator overnight.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Combine oil, minced garlic, salt and pepper together in a bowl. Clean heads of romaine lettuce; cut in half from end to end. Brush halved romaine lettuce with oil-garlic mixture.
  4. Add sliced peppers and onions to the oil-garlic mixture. Toss to combine.
  5. Arrange the romaine cut-side down on the oiled grill. Cook just until grill marks appear, about 2 minutes. Remove and set aside to cool.
  6. If you have a grill basket, spread marinated chicken evenly in the basket; otherwise, thread chicken and vegitables on skewers. Grill chicken for 5 minutes. Turn the chicken in the basket, add the vegitables, and grill until an instant-read thermometer inserted in the chicken reaches 165 degrees F (74 degrees C), about 10 minutes more.
  7. Slice grilled lettuce and place on serving plates. Divide chicken and vegitables evenly over the grilled romaine lettuce.
